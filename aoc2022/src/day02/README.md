### Rochambeau

Rock smashes Scissors
Paper covers Rock
Scissors cuts Paper

points for hand:
rock = 1, paper = 2, scissors = 3 

points for outcome:
win = 6, lose = 0, draw = 3

part 1: what to play to win
x = rock
y = paper
z = scissors

part 2: play for specific outcome
x = lose
y = draw
z = win
